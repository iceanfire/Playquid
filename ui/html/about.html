Meet our Team

You already know that at Playquid we're passionate about building social connections between co-workers in large organizations. But here's a bit more about our team & what drives us.

Hadi LaasiPlatform Development
Hadi builds the Playquid platform. He works with Nick & Dr. Fowler to meld enterprise & social science insights into an elegant solution. He's been designing & building on the web as far back as he can remember and absolutely loves it. Hadi has lived the corporate life for most of his career and understands well the challenges that workers face in creating valuable social ties--and is devoted to simplifying those interactions. In his free time, he loves to rock climb, play flag football and listen to music!


Nick ForanBusiness Development
Nick seeks new partnerships for Playquid. He also works to connect Playquid to highly relevant research on employee health and engagement. His roots are in the construction management industry and large team dynamics. This environment taught him that relationships sustain positive corporate culture. He believes a social approach that harnesses the power of technology is necessary to engage people in today's large organizations. He also spent an extended amount of time in South Africa. He counts his African friends and coworkers as the chief motivator in his work to overcome the challenges of poverty.


James FowlerScientific Advisor Dr. Fowler is a professor of political science and medical genetics at the University of California, San Diego. His work focuses on social network theory and behavioral economics. His research has been featured in the New York Times Magazine's Year in Ideas, Time's Year in Medicine, Discover Magazine's Year in Science, and Harvard Business Review's Breakthrough Business Ideas. Dr. Fowler along with Nicholas Christakis wrote the best-selling book Connected: The Surprising Power of Social Networks and How They Shape Our Lives. He joined the Playquid team to provide research-based direction for our technology products. Dr. Fowler shares our passion for improving the health and well-being of employees. His words on The Colbert Report and other contemporary broadcasts communicate the power of social networks and their ability to create positive change in the world.
